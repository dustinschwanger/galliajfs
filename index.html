  • Child & Adult Protective Services

    Emergency: To report child abuse or neglect, call 740-446-3222 Option 6 or the Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-422-4453. For adult abuse (age 60+), call 740-446-3222 Option 7. After hours, call 911.

    Child Protective Services

    Mission: To improve the safety, permanency, and well-being of children and families involved in the Child Welfare system through collaboration with families and their community.

    How to Report: Call Gallia County Children Services at 740-446-3222 Option 6 or the Child Abuse Hotline at 1-800-422-4453 and state you wish to make a child abuse and neglect report.

    Confidentiality: The law protects the confidentiality of a reporter. A social worker cannot reveal, confirm or deny who a reporter may be. There are no consequences for reports made in good faith, even if unfounded.

    Adult Protective Services

    Adult Protective Services staff investigate reports of abuse, neglect (including self-neglect) or financial exploitation of adults age 60 and over who are unable to protect themselves due to a serious physical or mental limitation.

    To Report Elder Abuse: Call 740-446-3222 Option 7

    Types of Elder Abuse:

    • Neglect: Caretakers failing to provide food, shelter, healthcare, or protection
    • Self-Neglect: Elderly individuals unable or unwilling to provide necessary self-care
    • Exploitation: Unauthorized use or theft of an elder's funds or property
    • Emotional Abuse: Verbal threats, intimidation, name-calling, harassment and isolation
    • Physical Abuse: Injury inflicted through hitting, kicking, or weaponry
    • Sexual Abuse: Nonconsensual or forced sexual contact

    Foster Care & Adoption

    Become a Foster Parent: Single or married, homeowners or renters, age 21+. Must be licensed with ongoing training and criminal check required.

    Adoption Requirements: Single or married, homeowners or renters, age 21+. If married, must have been married for at least one year.

    Contact: 740-446-3222 for foster care and adoption information.
